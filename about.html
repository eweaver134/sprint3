FAQ

What is our mission?

Our mission is to inform people about the various benefits adding robots to your company can offer. As technology advances, we understand that adding robots to your daily life can be scary. But with a team of robot experts, we hope to teach you how robots and humans can work together.

How can potential clients get in touch with Apple, The Fruit?

You can reach us by our phone number, 999-999-9999. You also have the option of filling out the information on the Contact Us Page so we can get in contact with you.

What sets your consulting company apart from other consulting companies?

Our company focuses on the people. We make sure to hire top-notch people highly educated in robotics to give you the best help we possibly can. With a consulting company based upon robotics, something that advances more and more every year, we also make sure we advance with it.

Who are the primary clients and audience for your consulting service?

Anyone with an open mind to learn about robots! We can offer advice on what robots would help your company. We can also help design specialized robots to help meet the specific needs of your business.

Do you offer ongoing or follow-up support after consultations?

Absolutely! We make sure our clients are 100% satisfied with our work. We make sure to frequently stay in contact with our clients and offer further advice or help where we can.
